DOG

At 8 weeks old, a dog’s personality is not yet developed enough to analyze!

  • Lifespan: 10 – 13 years
  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Daily sleep: 12 – 14 hours (Adult)
  • Height: 2.5 – 42 in

Dogs make the best pets and are fantastic companions. They are very loyal to their owners and provide a sense of unconditional love. Get a dog, and you'll have the greatest of friends!
